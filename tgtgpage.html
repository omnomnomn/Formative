Too Good To Go

The food waste app that took major cities by storm


In the last decade, households across the UK have been more aware of the impact of food waste and various government schemes have attempted to reduce this. In large cities, despite the dense populations, food waste is a major problem. In the UK, households throw away 4.5 million tonnes of edible food.

    Here are the countries with the most food waste:
  1. Belgium
  2. USA
  3. UK

Too Good To Go is an app developed to reduce food waste in the hospitality industry. Restaurants can sell their extra meals on the app at a discounted price to boost profits and reduce food waste. Customers can then search for restaurants about to close around them and buy delicious meals at a cheaper price.

    The company was founded in Copenhagen in 2015 and has globally saved over 51 million meals from being thrown away. Too Good To Go aims to:
  • inspire people to fight food Waste
  • make reducing food waste accessible to everyone
  • make food sustainability more enjoyable
